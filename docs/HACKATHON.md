**A:** "We implement end-to-end encryption, role-based access control, and comply with government data protection standards. In production, we'd use OAuth 2.0 and JWT tokens."

### Q: What about patients without smartphones?
**A:** "The platform is accessible via any device - smartphones, tablets, or computers at hospitals. We also plan SMS-based alerts for feature phones."
