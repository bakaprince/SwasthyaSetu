Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register with my ABHA ID?

Click on "Login/Register" and select "New User". Enter your 14-digit ABHA ID and follow the verification process via OTP.

How can I book an appointment?

After logging in, go to "Book Appointment", select your preferred hospital and doctor, choose available time slots, and confirm your booking.

Can I access my medical records from any hospital?

Yes, your digital health records are linked to your ABHA ID and can be accessed from any connected hospital in our network.

Is telemedicine consultation free?

Basic telemedicine consultations are free for government hospital doctors. Private consultations may have charges as per hospital policies.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your dashboard, click on "Profile Settings", make necessary changes, and verify updates via OTP for security.
